Education Data for Change

Change Education with Data!

Students in Ontario should be given an equal education independent of their location based on the Ontario School Board's mission. Education Data for Change lets you see if there should be change for your region.

1. Enter School

Find average EQAO provincial standard achievement rate for your school
The biggest determining factor for the percentage of students in a school who achieve the provincial standard for EQAO, is the percentage of students in low-income households for that school.
